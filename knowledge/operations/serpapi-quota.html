The full API stack — cost & limits

Total cash cost of the stack: £0/month. Everything except SerpApi is free and un-keyed; SerpApi is free-tier but quota-capped, which is why it gets the live meter above. Caches are committed to the repo so daily builds barely touch the rate-limited sources. Full failure behaviour per source: External APIs.
