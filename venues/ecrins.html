Écrins (Aiguille Dibona) — multi-pitch climbing

France · granite · alpine rock spire, hut-based multi-pitch · grades E1

The Dibona is the prettiest rock spire in the Alps — a clean granite blade with classic routes on every face and a comfortable hut an hour below. High-pressure late-July weather is the norm, and climbing at 3,000m keeps the heat civilised.
