Picos de Europa — multi-pitch climbing

Spain · limestone · moderate-to-hard alpine multi-pitch (Naranjo de Bulnes)

Alpine limestone in green northern Spain — the huge orange faces of the Naranjo de Bulnes are the objective, with a mountain hut right below the wall. Far cooler than southern Spain in late July, though Atlantic mists can roll in without notice. In climbing-trips.csv.
