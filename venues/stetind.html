Stetind (Norway) — multi-pitch climbing

Norway · granite · arctic granite big-ridge (Sydpillaren) · grades E1

Norway's national mountain: a 1,392m granite obelisk rising straight out of the fjord, with the classic Sydpillaren pillar as the prize. Late July means midnight sun — 24 hours of daylight and no bivvy stress — so weather is the only gatekeeper.
