Mournes, NI — multi-pitch climbing

Northern Ireland · granite · moderate multi-pitch trad · grades VD–VS

Compact granite domes and tors an hour from Belfast — friction slabs, clean cracks and long mountain days with the sea in view from every summit. Local to Dan, moderate grades, and a solid choice when the forecast only gives a short two-day window.
