Isle of Arran — multi-pitch climbing

Scotland · granite · mountain multi-pitch (Cìr Mhòr) · grades VS

Coarse-grained granite ridges and slabs rising straight from the sea — Cir Mhor's clean walls are the prize, and the Glasgow-ferry approach makes it feel like an expedition. Bring midge protection and a sense of humour about bog. In climbing-trips.csv as Aaran.
