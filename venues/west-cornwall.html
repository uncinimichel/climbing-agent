West Cornwall (Bosigran) — multi-pitch climbing

England · granite · immaculate sea-cliff trad (Bosigran, Chair Ladder) · grades HS

Golden granite above the Atlantic — Commando Ridge, Doorpost and Chair Ladder's tiered walls are England's finest sea-cliff trad. It is the warmest, driest corner of the UK in late July, and the rock dries in minutes in a breeze.
