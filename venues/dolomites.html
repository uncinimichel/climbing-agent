Dolomites (Cortina) — multi-pitch climbing

Italy · limestone · vast Full-Range multi-pitch · grades VD–HS

The confirmed backup if Ireland is wet: vast limestone faces around Cortina with multi-pitch routes of every length and grade, and rifugi that put you at the base of the climbs. Late July is peak season — warm rock and settled mornings, with the classic afternoon-thunderstorm rhythm to plan around. Routes already scoped in dolomites-trip.csv.
