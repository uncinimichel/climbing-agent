More climbing & guidebook resources

  • Tenerife, Rock climbing (area hub) (theCrag.com) — Central database of all Tenerife climbing areas, including Teide National Park, Guaria and other multi-pitch trad crags.
  • El Catedral crag logbook (UKClimbing.com) — Details the 2200m-altitude Teide National Park crag with 15 mostly-traditional 1-4 pitch granite/ignimbrite routes.
  • Guaria crag access and route info (TheTopo (formerly 27crags)) — Covers permit requirements, approach, and multi-pitch trad/sport climbing at Tenerife's tallest and most concentrated multi-pitch crag.
  • Multi-pitch climbing on the Teide volcano (DirectMountain) — Route-level beta on several Teide/Anaga multi-pitch trad and bolted lines (La Catedral, Roques de Guillermo, 7 Estrellas Verdes, La Directa Rusa).
  • Las Cañadas / Parque Nacional del Teide climbing guide (Canarias Climb) — Local Spanish-language guide to the Teide National Park sectors (El Capricho, Los Cachorros, La Catedral) and access via the Parador Nacional del Teide.
