More climbing & guidebook resources

  • Die Sächsischen Kletterregeln (The Saxon Climbing Rules) (Sächsischer Bergsteigerbund (SBB)) — Official rulebook explaining the free-climbing-only ethic: no bolts/cams/chalk, protection only via ring bolts and slings/knots, since 1913.
  • Tipps zum ersten Besuch im Elbsandstein (Sächsischer Bergsteigerbund (SBB)) — Practical guidance for newcomers on gear, protected-area etiquette, and recommending a guide before climbing independently.
  • Sächsische Schweiz (Saxon Switzerland), Trad Climbing (theCrag.com) — Area database covering the whole Saxon Switzerland region's crags, towers and thousands of routes.
  • Lines in the Sand - Crack Climbing on German & Czech Sandstone (UKClimbing) — Feature article on the shared Saxon/Bohemian sandstone tradition, explaining knotted-rope protection and chalk ban.
  • Elbsandstein Plaisir Climbing Guidebook (climbing-guide.eu) — Publisher/retailer page for a guidebook covering over 400 routes across five sub-areas of the Elbe Sandstone Mountains.
