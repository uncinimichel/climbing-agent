Snowdonia (Llanberis Pass) — multi-pitch climbing

Wales · rhyolite/dolerite · vast, Full-Range multi-pitch · grades D–E1

The home of classic UK mountain trad: Dinas Cromlech's open-book corners, Cloggy's big serious faces, Tryfan's endless ridges. Drivable and cheap — but rhyolite is slow to dry, so it needs a genuine dry spell to shine. In climbing-trips.csv as Llanberis.
