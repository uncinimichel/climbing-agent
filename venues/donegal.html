Donegal (Sail Rock) — multi-pitch climbing

Ireland · quartzite · sea cliffs & sea stacks (Cnoc na Mara) · grades VS · tidal access — plan around low water

Ireland's wild west: Sail Rock's perfect corner lines and the Cnoc na Mara sea-stack are as adventurous as trad gets, three hours' drive from Belfast. Remote, free and swell-dependent — a calm, dry window here beats almost anywhere.
