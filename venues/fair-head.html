Fair Head, NI — multi-pitch climbing

Northern Ireland · dolerite · long single & multi-pitch trad

The biggest expanse of climbable rock in Ireland — a sweep of towering dolerite columns above the sea, stacked with steep, juggy crack lines from VS upward. Dan lives down the road, so logistics are trivial: no hire car, no faff, just racks and the Causeway coast. Catches sea fog, but a breeze dries it fast.
