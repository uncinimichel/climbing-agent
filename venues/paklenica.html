Paklenica (Anica Kuk) — multi-pitch climbing

Croatia · limestone · big-wall multi-pitch up to 350m + sport

Velebit's big limestone canyon above the Adriatic: routes to 350m on Anica Kuk, bolted belays, and a swim across the road afterwards. Workable in July if you chase shade — the great NORTH face of Anica Kuk stays out of the sun and most crags face E/W. Season Apr–Oct (sources: climbgrades.com, mojagear.com).
